About Me

Monalisa's mission is to empower companies and individuals alike to innovate, grow, and lead in the rapidly changing world of technology. As a marketing expert and advisor, she has worked with global teams, delivered high-impact solutions, and cultivated thriving ecosystems around disruptive technologies.

Monalisa Agbata is a Global Marketing Executive (EMEA), creator, public speaker, and go-to-market strategist with extensive experience in early and growth-stage tech startups. She co-founded One Dev (formerly Memoi Africa), where she has built a thriving community of over 5,000 African developers, creating over 1,000 jobs and opportunities in emerging technologies like blockchain, Web3, and AI. Under her leadership, One Dev has attracted grants from major organizations such as the Ethereum Support Program, Lisk, and CELO.

Monalisa has held pivotal roles including Product Marketing Manager at Kvants AI, Regional Marketing Manager at COINW, and Director of Product Marketing at Zenith Chain, where she drove market penetration across Africa, LATAM, and APAC regions. With over eight years of experience in marketing, growth & business development, she has consistently positioned startups for rapid growth.

Monalisa holds a bachelor’s degree in Communication and an MBA from the Quantic School of Business and Technology, USA. Her value-driven approach, paired with her expertise in emerging technologies and marketing strategy, positions her as an invaluable asset to any organization she supports.
